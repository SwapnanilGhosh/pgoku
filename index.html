ABOUT GOKU

Goku is the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball franchise. He is a pure-hearted and courageous warrior who constantly strives to become stronger and protect his loved ones. Goku is a Saiyan, a race of powerful extraterrestrial warriors, and possesses incredible strength, speed, and endurance.


He is known for his signature move, the Kamehameha, and has faced numerous enemies throughout his adventures, including Frieza, Cell, and Majin Buu. Goku's unwavering determination and selflessness have made him a beloved character among fans of the series.
